Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwhich

Description

This will teach you how to properly make a peanut butter and jelly sandwhich!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Set out two pieces of bread
  2. Spread jelly with a knife on one skice
  3. Spread Peanut butter with a knife on the other
  4. Place both slices on top of each other
  5. Enjoy!
