Fried Rice

Description

This will teach you how to properly make fried rice!

Ingredients

  • White rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Mixed vegitables
  • Chicken
  • Your choice of Spices for Chicken

Steps

  1. Boil rice
  2. Cut and season chicken
  3. Dice and place vegitables in microwave safe bowl and fill with enough water to submerge veggies
  4. Heat veggies in microwave for 3 minutes
  5. Cook chicken how you would like
  6. Pour olive oil in pan and heat on stove, then place boiled rice in pan and begin frying
  7. Pour a half cup of soy sauce on rice and place veggies in with rice to start fying
  8. Once rice is done and a golden brown color, take chicken and place in with rice
  9. Enjoy!
